================================================================================

- JavaScript can be bypassed by advanced users (intentional)
- Cannot prevent all screen capture methods
- Primary protection is backend watermarking (LemonInk)
- JavaScript is supplementary only

Payment Processing:
- PayPal processing may take 2-3 hours during high volume
- International transfers limited to whitelisted countries
- Some payment methods require 3D Secure authentication

GDPR Compliance:
- Templates are starting point (lawyer review recommended)
- Company-specific customization required
- Regional variations may apply outside France
- CNIL registration required for France-based companies
